Proceedings of the Council of the City of Joliet, Illinois

held on March 7, 2017

VIDEO IS HEREIN INCORPORATED

INTO THE OFFICIAL MINUTES

DRAFT MINUTES SUBJECT TO

COUNCIL APPROVAL

Councilman Morris stated the Land Use and Legislative Committee met on

March 1, 2017 at 9:00 a.m. He stated representatives from Hawk Auto came

before the Committee proposing to build a new Subaru dealership at Route

59 and Theodore Street. He stated the Committee also

discussed Ordinances amending licenses giving the City's Mayor

authorization for emergency closure of businesses. He stated Alfredo

Melesio, Director of Neighborhood Services, discussed with the Committee

a bank-owned abandoned property registration Ordinance. He stated the

Committee also discussed abandoned above-ground swimming pool

regulations, update on the proposed dog Ordinance, absenteeism of

Council Members, temporary summer farm stands and potential expansion

of same, Ordinance prohibiting the sale, delivery, and possession of drug

paraphernalia, intergovernmental agreement with the Forest Preserve and

Village of Shorewood for connecting the path from Rock Run over I-55. He

stated the next meeting of the Land Use and Legislative Committee is

Monday, March 20, 2017 at 9:00 a.m.

Mayor and Corporation Council briefly discussed the proposed dog

Ordinance with regard to the pending litigation of a Chicago case to prevent

any law suits when the City adopts an Ordinance.

B. Public Service

Councilman Hug stated the Public Service Committee met yesterday

evening at 4:30 p.m. to discuss the 2017 deteriorated roadway resurfacing

projects. He stated the Committee also reviewed the contracts, change

orders, pay estimates, and final payments and found them all to be in order

and recommended their approval.
