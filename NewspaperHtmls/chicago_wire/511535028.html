Analysis: DePaul men’s head coaches’ pay ranks 91st in nation

Sports

By LGIS Newsfeed | Aug 24, 2018

Head coaches of DePaul University's men’s teams earn an average of $422,068 annually – the 91st highest average salary in the nation– according to a Chicago City Wire analysis of federal education data.

The analysis found that the university had the equivalent of 4.5 full-time head coaches on staff for its men’s teams. 

The U.S. Department of Education’s office of postsecondary education collects athletics data annually from all institutions with intercollegiate athletics programs that receive funding through federal financial aid programs. Data for 2016 – the latest year available – includes more than 2,000 schools nationwide.
