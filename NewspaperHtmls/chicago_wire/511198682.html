Top earner at Chicago State University brought home $215K in 2016

By LocalLabs News Service | Aug 29, 2017

Senior Vice President for Academic Affairs Angela Henderson was the highest-earning individual at Chicago State University in 2016, with total compensation reaching $215,625, according to a Chicago City Wire analysis of Illinois Board of Higher Education data. 

The 100 highest-earning administrators and faculty members were paid a total of $11,398,493.29 in 2016.

CSU President Wayne Watson was the second-highest earning individual, with $191,188 in 2016, followed by College of Arts & Science Dean Leroy Jones II ($172,441) Associate Dean Carmita Coleman ($159,794), and College of Business Dean Derrick Collins ($152,502).
