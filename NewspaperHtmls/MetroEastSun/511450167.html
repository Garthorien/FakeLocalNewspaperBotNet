O'FALLON CHAMBER OF COMMERCE: MCIDEATIVE Holds a Ribbon Cutting!

By Press release submission | Jun 8, 2018

O'Fallon Chamber of Commerce issued the following announcement on June 6.

The O’Fallon-Shiloh Chamber of Commerce joined MCIDEATIVE as they celebrated their grand opening! They are located at 220 East State Street, O’Fallon, Suite 1D. They celebrated with a ribbon cutting ceremony on May 23rd.

MCIDEATIVE design studio is an intimate creative space. From the creations of artisan products, mixed media, visual styling to events. This studio provokes curiosity and inspires ideation.

The space also serves as a showroom to the vintage & handcrafted goods of BEAUTIFi + KAZARU where purchases can be made. Open to events and creative co-working. MCIDEATIVE offers creative services in design, branding & photography.

Marianeth, the owner, is excited to pursue her creative endeavors after serving time in the military and strives to grow as an entrepreneur, all while contributing to social impact work and traveling the world. She is now fusing her love of business and design by pursuing an MBA in Design Strategy from San Francisco. MCIDEATIVE is a proud veteran owned business.

For more information go to: WWW.MCIDEATIVE.COM | 1.888.415.9707
