O'FALLON CHAMBER OF COMMERCE: Jill Kinsella, DMV, Celebrates New Location!

By Press release submission | Jun 15, 2018

O'Fallon Chamber of Commerce issued the following announcement on June 13.

The O’Fallon-Shiloh Chamber of Commerce joined Jill Kinsella, DMV, as she celebrated her new office location at 703 Seibert Road, Ste 5 (just outside of Scott AFB). 

She celebrated with a ribbon cutting ceremony on June 1st.

The dental office of Jill Kinsella DMD moved to its new location on May 14, 2018. 

Dr. Kinsella joined Dr. Robert Parish’s practice in 2007 as an associate serving military families as well as members of the surrounding communities and businesses. 

She purchased the practice in January 2012 with the dream of having a dental practice where her patients and her team are treated like family. 

Dr. Kinsella and her team make it a priority to provide gentle, compassionate dental care.
