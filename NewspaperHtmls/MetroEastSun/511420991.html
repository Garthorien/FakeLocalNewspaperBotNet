O'FALLON CHAMBER OF COMMERCE: MP Ice, LLC, Celebrates!

Business

By Press release submission | May 19, 2018

O'Fallon Chamber of Commerce issued the following announcement on May 14.

The O’Fallon-Shiloh Chamber of Commerce joined MP Ice, LLC, as they celebrated the opening of their new ice station at the North end of the St. Clair Bowl parking lot. 

They are located at 5950 Old Collinsville Road. They celebrated with a ribbon cutting ceremony on April 19th.

The ice vending machine offers 20-pound bulk ice and 16-pound bag ice as well as filtered water by the gallon or 5-gallon at wholesale prices. The machine is open 24-hours a day 7 days a week. Give it a try!
