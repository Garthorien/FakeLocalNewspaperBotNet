O'FALLON CHAMBER OF COMMERCE: Morrison Plumbing, Heating & Air Celebrates Expansion!

By Press release submission | Jun 11, 2018

O'Fallon Chamber of Commerce issued the following announcement on June 6.

The O’Fallon-Shiloh Chamber of Commerce joined Morrison Plumbing, Heating & Air as they celebrated the expansion of their business as well as a new location! They are now located at 8124B Bunkum Road, Caseyville. They celebrated with a ribbon cutting ceremony on May 16th.

For over 30 years, the Morrison name has been providing services to the O’Fallon/Shiloh and metro-east areas. Morrison Plumbing, Heating and Air is fully licensed, bonded and insured with the state of Illinois. Our Service Experts and Apprentices are licensed with the state and receive continuous training in order to provide our clients with the most up to date solutions and services for their homes.

Besides plumbing, heating and air conditioning services, Morrison provides backflow prevention, drain cleaning services and is a Luxury Bath dealer. With the many services provided, why call anyone else?

Morrison Plumbing, Heating and Air specializes in providing our customers with fast, friendly professional services. We will effectively handle your repair by one of our professional service experts. You will get what you’re asking for and receive 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Our goal is to provide clean and helpful services that our clients will be happy with. Utilizing our many services means that you are getting great customer service. Customer service is how we continue to build our reputation in the area.

Morrison Plumbing, Heating and Air is the FAMILY business you want to call upon. From the caring and compassionate customer service representatives to the knowledgeable service experts, our team of employees truly CARE about our customers and clients.

Find Morrison Plumbing, Heating and Air on Facebook and at morrisonplumbing.com
