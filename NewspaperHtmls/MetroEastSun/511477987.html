O'FALLON CHAMBER OF COMMERCE: Osborne Chiropractic Welcomes Dr. Brandon Daniels to the Practic

O'Fallon Chamber of Commerce issued the following announcement on July 5.

Dr. Corey Osborne and the staff at Osborne Family Chiropractic would like to welcome Dr. Brandon Daniels to the practice! Dr. Daniels was born and raised in O'Fallon and recently graduated Cum

Laude with his Doctorate of Chiropractic from Logan University in Chesterfield, MO. In March 2018, he joined the OFC family. Dr. Daniels specializes in Acupuncture and Applied Kinesiology and will soon begin his training in Functional Medicine.

Osborne Family Chiropractic opened in 2002 and is a Chiropractic and Functional Medicine practice. For more information about Dr. Daniels and Osborne Family Chiropractic, please visit our website at www.osbornefamilychiro.com.
