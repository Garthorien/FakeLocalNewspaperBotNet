Rockford University has 35th highest number of student athletes in Illinois

Sports

By LGIS Newsfeed | Dec 28, 2018

Rockford University ranked No. 35 for student athletic participation in the state, according to a Rockford Sun analysis of college sports participation.

The U.S. Department of Education Office of Postsecondary Education collects athletics data annually from all institutions with intercollegiate athletics programs that receive funding through federal financial aid programs. Data for 2016 – the latest year available – includes more than 2,000 schools nationwide.

The number of Rockford University students taking part in athletic programs was 243 – 183 men and 60 women – representing the largest contingent of student athletes in Illinois..

In all, there are 23,295 students – 14,354 men and 8.941 women – playing on athletic teams at 97 colleges and universities in the state.
