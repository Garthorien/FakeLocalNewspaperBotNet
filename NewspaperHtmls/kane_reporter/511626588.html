Analysis: Aurora University posts 49th highest gender pay gap for head coaches in Illinois

Sports

By LGIS Newsfeed | Nov 19, 2018

Men’s head coach salaries at Aurora University outpaced women’s team coaches by 11 percent, according to a Kane County Reporter analysis of recent U.S. Department of Education data.

Male head coaches at the university earn an average of $65,409, while female head coaches’ annual pay is $59,039. 

The U.S. Department of Education’s office of postsecondary education collects athletics data annually from all institutions with intercollegiate athletics programs that receive funding through federal financial aid programs. Data for 2016 – the latest year available – includes more than 2,000 schools nationwide.

A total of 96 universities and colleges in Illinois paid salaries to both men’s and women’s head coaches in 2016.
