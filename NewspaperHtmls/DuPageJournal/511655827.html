Analysis: Average Hinsdale SD 86 teacher comp is $154K

By LGIS News Service | Dec 6, 2018

Editor's note: The original version of this story erroneously included payroll information for two Hinsdale area school districts.

The average teacher at Hinsdale SD 86 received $153,978 in total compensation during the 2015-2016 school year for working nine months, according to an analysis of Illinois State Board of Education (ISBE) and Teachers Retirement System (TRS) records by the Dupage Policy Journal.

The analysis covers 480 district employees who received a collective $73.9 million in 2015-2016.

The total compensation number was calculated by combining base salary for nine months, cost of benefits (at 30 percent), paid sick days and taxpayer-funded pension contributions for each employee.

The pension contribution was 37 percent of each employee's base salary in 2016, according to TRS.

Public school employees pay into the state pension system approximately 3 percent of what they stand to collect in retirement, or $30,000 for each $1 million received. Taxpayers make up the difference.
