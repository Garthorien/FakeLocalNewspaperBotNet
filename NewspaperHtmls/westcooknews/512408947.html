OAK PARK-RIVER FOREST CHAMBER OF COMMERCE: Cultural Experience Camp

Oak Park-River Forest Chamber of Commerce issued the following announcement on March 28.

Have FUN exploring Afro-Brazilian songs, dances, games and folklore!

The program opens with a creation story starring the African instrument Kalimba (Thumb piano), followed by a week of games, dances, percussion and rhythms, singing and exposure to Brazilian and African culture. August 2nd there will be a performance and celebration!

Dates: July 29th-August 2nd

Times: 1:30pm - 4:00pm

Location: TBA - Oak Park, IL

Ages: 8 – 13

Price: $170.00 per child

(Price includes light snack, materials, and a final performance)
