Top earner at U of I at Springfield brought home $306K in 2016

By LocalLabs News Service | Aug 31, 2017

Distinguished Professor Michael Hogan was the highest-earning individual at University of Illinois at Springfield in 2016, with total compensation reaching $306,269 according to a Sangamon Sun analysis of Illinois Board of Higher Education data. 

The 100 highest-earning administrators and faculty members were paid a total of $10,904,934.18 in 2016.

Chancellor of U of I at Springfield and Vice President Susan Koch was the second-highest earning individual, with $244,128 in 2016, followed by Dean Ronald McNeil ($198,337), Vice Chancellor Patricia Pardie ($193,867), and Dean JAmes Ermatinger ($184,925).
