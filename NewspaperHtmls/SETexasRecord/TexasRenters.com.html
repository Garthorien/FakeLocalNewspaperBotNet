TopnotchServices

We provide the full range of property management services, from screening ‘could be’ tenants, to service and maintenance calls, as well as handling all of your financial responsibilities like collecting and paying rent. Our goal is to have your property rented within 30 days with qualified, solid tenants. We have been serving the Houston, Texas market since 2011, and as property owners ourselves, we understand the real estate market in Houston and all the economics of property ownership, leasing and maintaining properties.
