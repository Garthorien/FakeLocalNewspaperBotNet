City of Beaumont Christmas Tree Lighting to Kick Off 180 Celebration

City of Beaumont Christmas Tree Lighting to Kick Off 180 Celebration

(Beaumont, TX – October 24, 2018) — The City of Beaumont will kick off its year-long 180th Birthday Celebration with the largest Christmas tree lighting in Beaumont at the Downtown Event Centre Lake, Wednesday, December 5 at 5:30pm.

This free holiday event offers entertainment and fun for the entire family, including live music, food trucks, fireworks and an outdoor movie. The evening will conclude with a showing of holiday classic, Frosty’s Winter Wonderland, on a large, outdoor screen.

Admission to the event is free and open to the public.

Beaumont was established in 1838 and began its beautiful story rich in culture, industry, natural resources and incredible talent. This exciting holiday celebration will serve as the kickoff for the City of Beaumont’s 180 Celebration, which will commemorate the city’s 180th Birthday. The year-long celebration will begin on December 5 and continue with various events and activities throughout the community.

For up to date event information visit discoverbeaumont.com, follow Beaumont Events on social media or call 409.838.3435. Download the entertainment app on your mobile device by searching “Discover Beaumont” on Google Play and the App Store.
