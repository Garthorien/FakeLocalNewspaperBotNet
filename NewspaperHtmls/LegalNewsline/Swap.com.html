What is Swap.com?

We enable a community of thrifters to find affordable, quality secondhand apparel for the whole family. Being an online thrift store, we make it easier than ever to filter through like-new, pre-owned clothing. Together we keep millions of items out of landfills which is something everyone can feel good about.

Hand Inspected Quality

We hand inspect every item to ensure they're ready for secondhand use. We constantly hear positive reviews from customers on our quality — even on those noted "fair condition".

Affordable Pricing

Find up to 90% off retail pricing on your favorite brands. Unlock the secret to feel-good fashion at an affordable cost. People won't know, but your wallet will!

Hassle-Free Shipping

Doesn't fit? Aren't in love? No worries! Send it back within 30 days and we will take care of the return shipping. Plus, we always offer free shipping on orders over $40!
